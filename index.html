About Me

Hi, I'm Victor Araújo — a creative designer passionate about solving complex problems through human-centered experiences. With a degree in Computer Science from UFPE (CIn) and a lifelong love for drawing, I bring together strategy and creativity to craft digital experiences that matter.

With over 12 years of experience, I work at the intersection of product design, branding, and front-end development — turning challenges into functional, beautiful, and intuitive solutions. I love being involved in the entire design process: from user research to product delivery.

Outside of work, I’m driven by curiosity — whether it's learning new skills or exploring new places — and I truly believe in the power of design to bridge technology and emotion.

Let’s connect and bring great ideas to life!
